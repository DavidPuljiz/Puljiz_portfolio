Robotics Engineer with over 10 years of experience in developing novel Human-Robot Interaction methods through the use of Mixed Reality, Head-Mounted Displays and Machine Vision. I have worked on numerous projects and supervised over 15 Bachelor and Master students.

## Experience

### Research Assistant
**Institute for Robotics and Autonomous Systems, Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences (HKA), Germany**  
*10/2022 - 09/2024*

- Project [RoLe4D - "Robot Learning in 4-Dimensions"](https://www.h-ka.de/en/iaf/role4d)
- Simplified teaching of robotics through virtual reality environments, gamification concepts and intuitive data visualization
- Co-authored 3 research papers about the use of mixed reality in industrial and educational settings
- Prepared and conducted numerous project demonstrations in the "XR Lab" of the University

### Research Assistant
**Intelligent Process Automation and Robotics Lab, Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany**  
*06/2014 - 09/2022*

- Worked on projects including [ReApp](https://ipr.iar.kit.edu/english/315_1863.php) and [SafeLog](https://ipr.iar.kit.edu/projekte_2396.php)
- Focused on Augmented Reality, Human-Robot Interaction, Machine Vision, Vision-guided robot control
- Developed an end-to-end portable system for human-robot interaction based on the Microsoft HoloLens
- Supervised 16 Bachelor and Master students
- Published 15 peer-reviewed papers in venues including IROS, HRI and RCIM
